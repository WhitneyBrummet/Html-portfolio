The Best Movies According to Whitney

My top 3 movies of all-time.

How To Train Your Dragon

A story about vikings taming and riding dragons. This is my favorite movie because I watched it with my mom on our day out alone together in IMAX 3D. I also like the compelling story of someone who doesn't fit in to normal society finding their own path.

Bolt

You can't go wrong with Disney and you definitely can't go wrong with a dog with superpowers. I like the way this movie shows the bond between a dog and human.

Spirit

Favorite childhood movie.
